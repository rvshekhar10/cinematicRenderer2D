## Cinematic Principles Applied

### 1. **Varied Pacing**
- Not all scenes are the same length
- Creates natural rhythm and prevents monotony
- Faster scenes (2.4-2.5s) alternate with slower scenes (2.8-3.0s)

### 2. **Transition Speed Matches Effect**
- **Fast transitions** (800-900ms): Slide, Wipe - energetic, directional
- **Medium transitions** (1000-1300ms): Crossfade, Zoom, Blur - balanced
- **Slow transitions** (1400ms): Dissolve - complex, needs appreciation

### 3. **Opening and Closing**
- **Scene 1** (2.8s): Moderate opening - sets professional tone
- **Scene 5** (3.0s): Longest scene - builds to climax
- **Scene 6** (2.7s): Strong finish before loop

### 4. **Total Duration**
- **16 seconds** total - perfect for a demo loop
- Long enough to showcase all transitions
- Short enough to maintain attention
- Loops smoothly back to start
